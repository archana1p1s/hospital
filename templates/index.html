CITY HOSPITAL is a leading healthcare institution renowned for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced medical technology, and a team of highly skilled professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional care. As a multispecialty hospital, it provides a wide range of services, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and pediatrics, ensuring comprehensive care under one roof. The hospital is committed to a patient-centered approach, offering personalized treatment plans, round-the-clock support, and cutting-edge facilities like robotic-assisted surgery and advanced diagnostics. With a strong focus on research and innovation, it actively collaborates with global organizations to stay at the forefront of medical advancements. Guided by its mission to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable, CITY HOSPITAL serves as a beacon of trust and excellence, redefining standards in the healthcare industry.

Message from Chairman

Driven by passion to create benchmarks in the field of healthcare, BMH was formed in 1987 as a modest 52 bed facility. We had a dream to meet the growing healthcare needs of people in the region and a mission to make superior healthcare facilities available to the masses at affordable cost.


With the blessings of the Almighty and the relentless commitment by a team of professionals along with following ethical practices in every realm of healthcare and the unstinted support and patronage of our patients, BMH is now a multi specialty, tertiary care, referral hospital, running as a Public Limited Company.
